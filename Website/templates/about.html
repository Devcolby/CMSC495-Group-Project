My name is Sairam Soundararajan and I am a University of Maryland Global Campus student majoring in Computer Science. Aside from my academic interests, I also enjoy composing music, singing, rapping, and playing the piano/keyboard. I also strongly believe in spirituality in my hindu culture. I feel like my spirituality guides me in my daily life, especially mentally. I enjoy sports such as baseball, basketball, badminton, tennis, archery(bows and arrows), and bowling. I enjoy watching dance performances such as Bharatanatyam(South Indian Classical Dance), street dance, ballet, many forms of modern dance, and doing dances like foxtrot, tango, jitterbug, and any romantic dances. I want to be with a girl who is kind, nonjudgemental, spirited, funny, amiable, honest, caring, and receptive. I want her to like me for who I am as person morally, not what my achilles heel or conditions are.
